Kit Number 5944 Price $25
Subject Modeled 2 Staffel, KGzbV 1, Milos, Greece, 1941
Start Date
Complete Date February 2006
This was one of the long list of warbirds of World War II that eluded me as a child. It was one that I truly wanted to build way back when. So flashing forward many years, when I saw this kit on a hobby shop shelf, I of course snagged it.
This was my first effort at weathering painted surfaces, which is visible in the one picture I have of the "Tante Ju", the nicknamed the Germans bestowed on this versatile transport aircraft.
The Ju52 currently hangs in my garage and is covered, I'm sure, by a thick coat of dust and cob-webs. I hope to pull it down, clean it up, and shoot several more photographs. And one final thing that I notice in the photograph is the date, which indicates that within a month my daughter was going to come into this world.
