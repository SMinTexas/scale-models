Nakajima B5N2 "Kate"




Kit Nakajima B5N2 Type 97 Carrier Attack Bomber "Kate" Model 3 "Folding Wing"
Manufacturer Hasegawa Scale 1/48
Kit Number 9553 Price $35
Subject Modeled Sorye first attack wave, Pearl Harbor, December 7, 1941
Start Date October 8, 2018
Complete Date December 7, 2018
Yes, that completion date was intended. I had dubbed the year 2018 as my "Year of the Axis Build", with the goal of completing each of the primary aircraft involved in the December 7th attack on Pearl Harbor by December 7, 2018. I was able to do just that, with three builds of the D3A1 "Val", B5N2 "Kate", and a Zero that technically was not the correct variant for the date (but I had no plans to purchase an era-correct kit).
When I bought this kit more than a decade ago, I had intended to build the "Kate" with the wings folded. But as the time to actually build her approached, I could not get past the notion that I like to look at aircraft in their natural state - with the wings fully extended. So even though the kit included a pricey upgrade, I opted to leave all the wing-fold parts off and build her ready for flight.
